Spring 2024 was a challenging and interesting time to be a NYU student.
These are some of the photos I clicked and was able to recover from protests around campus.

Different Worlds. A NYU grad couple posing in front of a NYU Palestine protest.

A solitary person protesting in front of Gould Plaza after mass arrests the night before.
In the background, NYU is installing barriers to prevent the reformation of the encampment.

Anger and frustration seeped through the student body across campus. The once safe haven had suddenly transformed into a place clouded by fear.

Jews for NYU students.

NYU banned tents when the second encampment was formed. To protect the encampment from the rain, NYU students covered themselves up on a cold night with tarps.
